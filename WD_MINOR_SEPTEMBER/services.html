Frequently Asked Questions

What is the highest package of NIT Srinagar?

The highest package for the year 2020 stood at INR 22 LPA whereas the average package stood at INR 13 LPA.

How can I get NIT in Srinagar?

For admission to MSc programmes at NIT Srinagar, candidates need to appear for IIT JAM followed by CCMN Counselling. NIT Srinagar MBA admission 2021 is done basis a candidate's performance in CAT/CMAT followed by a Group Discussion and Personal Interview rounds.

Is Srinagar safe?

All the tourist places like Srinagar, Gulmarg, Sonamarg, and Pahalgam are safe to visit. They said that no one attacks to any tourist in Kashmir and they respect tourist as the god.

How is NIT Srinagar civil engineering?

It has above-average placements with high packages. Around 80-90% of students got placed in CS and EC branches. Around 80% of students got placed in IT, and 40-50% of students got placed in core branches like civil, mechanical, and electrical. The highest package offered was 20 LPA, and the lowest package was 5 LPA.

Is NIT Srinagar good for mechanical engineering?

It is the best college for engineering, especially its campus all over India. Placements: About 100% of students get placed from here, but due to Covid-19, lots of hardworking students didn't get placed from our department. However, from CSE and IT departments, almost most of the students got placed.

Is NIT Srinagar a good college?
